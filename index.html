About Me

Who Am I?

Hi! My name is Brian Wu, and I am a freshman at Stanford University majoring in Engineering Physics on the Computational Sciences Track. For the past three years, I conducted extensive self-directed research in planetary science, utilizing the Doppler spectroscopy technique to discover new exoplanets as well as developing software and Artificial Intelligence solutions that provide a more precise and efficient method to detect these exoplanets. My research primarily focused on a Tatooine-like circumbinary planet (a planet orbiting two stars), the first of its kind to be identified using Doppler spectroscopy.

I'm currently serving as Co-Founder and CEO of Vitol, a drone delivery company specializing in medical and humanitarian relief efforts. The Coronavirus pandemic not only demonstrated the inequities we as a society face in healthcare, but also the need for a more efficient transportation solution that minimizes human contact. Vitol seeks to accomplish exactly that - the future of transportation lies in the aerial realm, so let's build roads in the sky, together.

I'm highly skilled in data analytics and Machine Learning using MATLAB, Python, and IDL (Interactive Data Language). Additionally, I am also proficient in a wide variety of 3D Modeling techniques (primarily utilizing SolidWorks, AutoCAD/Inventor/Fusion 360, and Blender) as well as Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD), having built the flight systems analysis and airfoil flow model simulation software for Vitol's first prototype drone.

Aside from engineering and piloting delivery drones, running a startup, as well as college classes, I love to spend my time as active as I can be! Whether it's skiing, squash, basketball, road running, or bodybuilding, there's always something fun to do as long as you're active. I spend a lot of my free time experimenting with music production, and am currently working towards releasing select covers and original works on SoundCloud. Recently, I've been getting super into stock trading via RobinHood, so feel free to reach out if you'd like to have a virtual coffee chat about our common interests!

I'm always on the lookout for new challenges and opportunities for personal growth, as well as applying my talents in Engineering, Software Development, Research, and Entrepreneurship to world-changing causes. If you think I'd be a great fit for your mission, I'm more than happy to chat at brian.wu@stanford.edu!
